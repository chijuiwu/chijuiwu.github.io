I'm an English teaching assistant in Hualien, Taiwan. I will begin my Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) PhD on Cross-Device Interaction at Interactive Systems Research Group, Lancaster University, in January, 2018. I have worked closely with Dr. David Harris-Birtill at St Andrews Computer-Human Interaction Research Group (SACHI), Dr. Nicolai Marquardt and Dr. Steven Houben at University College London Interaction Centre (UCLIC). My background is in Computer Science, and my current research interests include sensing technologies, ubiquitous computing, and proxemic interaction. I build novel supporting infrastructures and techniques for ad hoc cross-device interaction.
