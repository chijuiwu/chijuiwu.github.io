I am a first-year Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) PhD student at Lancaster University, UK, supervised by Dr. Steven Houben. I am interested in Ubiquitous Computing, Interactive Spaces, and Cross-Device Interaction. I build novel supporting infrastructures and interaction techniques for ad hoc cross-device ecologies. Previously, I have worked closely with Dr. David Harris-Birtill at St Andrews Computer-Human Interaction Research Group (SACHI) and Dr. Nicolai Marquardt at University College London Interaction Centre (UCLIC). My background is in Computer Science and Machine Learning.
