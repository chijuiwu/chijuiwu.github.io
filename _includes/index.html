I am a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher interested in human-centered computing, humanistic psychology, and education. My research experience includes cross-device interaction, proxemic interaction, and accessibility. I was a HCI PhD student in the Interactive Systems group at Lancaster University, under the supervision of Dr. Steven Houben. Previously, I built sensor-based interactive systems with Dr. Nicolai Marquardt at University College London Interaction Centre (UCLIC) and Dr. David Harris-Birtill at St Andrews HCI Research Group (SACHI).
