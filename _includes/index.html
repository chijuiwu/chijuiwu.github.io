Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher in Taiwan. I am interested in human-centered computing, humanistic psychology, and education. My background is in computer science and machine learning (from St Andrews and UCL respectively), and my research experience includes cross-device interaction, proxemic interaction, and accessibility. I am currently learning about finance in my spare time. I was a HCI PhD student at Lancaster University and an English teaching assistant at Hualien Wanrong Junior High School.
